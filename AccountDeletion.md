# Account Deletion Instructions

  ## How to delete your DiabiPets account and data

  1. Open the DiabiPets app
  2. Go to **Settings** (gear icon in the top right)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the screen
  4. Tap **Delete Account** (red text)
  5. Confirm deletion in the dialog

  ## What gets deleted:
  - Your account information
  - All pet profiles
  - All glucose, insulin, and meal records
  - Cloud backup data (if you were signed in)

  ## Data deletion is permanent
  Once deleted, your data cannot be recovered.
